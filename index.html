Kjhovey Rebrand

Hovey is a personality on the streaming platform Twitch. Hovey's original brand, a pixel image of Link, evolved from his love of the game The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past. When designing his brand I wanted to incorporate elements from the game but give him something that was also his own. This was a special project because I also composed the song for the logo reveal.

You can check out kjhovey and his new branding on Twitch.

Destiny Sets Logo

destinysets.com is a community made inventory tool for Destiny The Game. The design forms an "S" while playing on the concept of the site's use of tiles in it's user interface.

Hostility Brand and Website

Hostility is a clan in Destiny The Game. Clans are Organized groups of players that regularly play together in one or more games. The name and design are meant as a parody of more serious competative clans. In addition to designing the logo I also coded and designed the clan's website hostilityclan.com.

Destiny Item Manager Logo

Destiny Item Manager (DIM) is a widely used, community built, inventory management tool for players of Destiny The Game. The DIM logo encompasses the purpose of the application which is to have your items with you at all times in an easy and manageable format. For this project I also animated a frame by frame reveal video and composed the accompanying music.

You can check out DIM at destinyitemmanager.com
