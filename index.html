Kjhovey Rebrand

Hovey's original brand was a pixel image of Link from A Link to the Past that evolved from his love of the game. So naturally when designing his own brand I wanted to incorporate those themes but give him something that was also his own. This was a special project because I also composed a song for the logo reveal.
